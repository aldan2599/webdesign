What are the best web hosts?

We will need to find a good web host. We will use the criteria to judge three websites. We will look at two web hosts (Squarespace and Wix) that require payment. We will then look at a free web host service (000Webhost.com).

Criteria

  1. Supports PHP and MySQL
  2. Offer e-commerce capabilities
  3. Provide at least 1 GB disk space
  4. Setup fees
  5. Monthly fees
  6. Domain name registration costs
  7. Amount of disk space
  8. Amount of traffic allowed
  9. Type and cost of e-commerce package

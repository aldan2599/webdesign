Squarespace

Criteria Answers
Supports PHP and MySQL: No
Provide at least 1GB disk space: Squarespace relies on Google Suite to host their client's products.
Customers are directed towards buying Google Suite products to accomodate their business needs.
However, certain pages have certain limits in terms of content.
For example,"event page support up to 350 events per month and while folders don't have offical page limits, Squarespace recommends 10 pages to keep drop down pages to a minimum."
Setup Fees: There are no setup fees.
However, there is a sevice fee which depends on the product chosen. Squarespace offers a Personal, Business, Basic Commerce, and Advanced Commerce Package.
Monthly fees: Depending on the chosen product, Squarespaces' monthly fees range from $16 to $46 per month.
There is a discount for yearly fees. Clients can save up to 30% by paying annually.
Domain Name registration costs: It is free, but only for one year. After that, standard rates apply.
Amount of disk space: Please see "Provide at least 1GB" for a more in-depth discussion.
Amount of traffic allowed: Squarespace claims their bandwidth and storage are unlimited and available for all products.
Type and Cost of E-commerce package: The Personal package does not support E-commerce.
The Business package offers fully integrated E-commerce package, but takes 3% of all transactions.
The Basic and Advanced Commerce packages offer fully integrated E-commerce packages, and do not have a transaction fee.

It should be noted that Squarespace has limited design capabilities. This is a website host for people who wish to quickly make a website
without having to learn the intricacies of HTML5, CSS, etc.

Wix

Criteria Answers
Supports PHP and MySQL: No, it uses Corvid, it's own language.
Provide at least 1GB disk space: Yes. Even the cheapest plan, the Combo, offers 3GB of storage.
While Wix does offer a free option,
there is only 500 MB of storage.
Setup Fees: There are no setup fees.
Monthly fees: Depending on the chosen product, Wix's monthly fees range from $13 to $39 per month.
Their packages include Combo ($13), Unlimited($17) Pro($22), and VIP($39). There is a discount for yearly fees.
Clients can get $5 off each month if they choose the yearly plan.
Domain Name registration costs: It is free for the first year ONLY if you pay with a yearly plan.
Otherwise, it's $14.95 a year.
Amount of disk space: The Combo plan, the cheapest at $13 a month, offers 3GB of storage.
The Unlimited offers 10GB, Pro(US Only) and VIP have 20GB.
Amount of traffic allowed: For the Combo plan, they offer 2GB of bandwidth per month. That's around 1,500 visitors allowed per month.
The Unlimited, Pro, and VIP plans offer unlimited bandwidth.
Type and Cost of E-commerce package: The Combo does not support E-commerce.
The Unlimited package offers Ad vouchers, a Site Booster App (like an SEO) and a visitor Analytics App.
The Pro package offers all of Unlimited offers plus and Events Calendar, Professional Logo, and Social Medial Logo files.
The VIP package offers all of those, but offers more technical support.
